auto_awesome Clinical Insight Synthesis

Recent large-scale clinical trials, most notably the SELECT trial [1], have demonstrated that GLP-1 receptor agonists (GLP-1 RAs) significantly reduce the risk of major adverse cardiovascular events (MACE) in patients both with and without type 2 diabetes.

Key findings indicate a 20% reduction in the composite primary endpoint of cardiovascular death, nonfatal myocardial infarction, or nonfatal stroke over a mean follow-up of 39.8 months. The benefit appears early and is sustained, suggesting mechanisms beyond simple glycemic control, including anti-inflammatory effects and direct vascular modulation [2].
